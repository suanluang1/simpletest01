A Royal Legacy

Suanluang Rama IX, also referred to as King Rama IX Park, is Bangkok's largest public park spanning 500 rai (80 hectares) in the Prawet District. Established in 1987 to commemorate King Bhumibol Adulyadej's (Rama IX) 60th birthday, this botanical paradise was officially opened by the King himself on December 1, 1987.

More than just a park, Suanluang Rama IX is a living tribute to the late King's dedication to environmental conservation and botanical research. The park features extensive botanical gardens, international-themed gardens, a serene lake, and pavilions showcasing the King's environmental initiatives.

Today, the Suan Luang Rama IX Foundation manages this green sanctuary, ensuring it remains a place of education, recreation, and natural beauty for generations to come.

Explore Our Gardens

Suanluang Rama IX features a diverse collection of gardens, each with its own unique theme and plant collections, offering visitors a journey through botanical wonders from around the world.

Chinese Garden

Features a wooden pavilion hidden in a walled garden, complete with lily ponds and tiny ornamental bridges reflecting traditional Chinese garden design.

Learn more

Japanese Garden

A peaceful garden featuring traditional Japanese elements including stone lanterns, carefully pruned trees, and tranquil water features designed for contemplation.

Learn more

Thai Botanical Garden

Home to an extensive collection of native Thai trees, tropical flowers, orchids, and rare plant species housed in special greenhouses.

Learn more

Italian Garden

Features ornate fountains and beautifully maintained rows of hedges in formal geometric patterns, showcasing European landscaping traditions.

Learn more

American Garden

Situated inside a dome reminiscent of the Nevada desert with sand and cacti, showcasing the diverse plant adaptations of arid environments.

Learn more

Water Park & Boardwalks

Explore lily ponds and swamp forest via wooden boardwalks, offering close observation of aquatic plants and diverse birdlife.

Available Activities

  • Paddleboat rentals on the lake (swan and duck boats)
  • Small train tours around popular sites

  • Outdoor gym and exercise equipment
  • Photography tours through the gardens

Facilities & Services

  • Cafés and refreshment stalls throughout the park
  • Food vendors near entrances offering Thai snacks
  • Public restrooms (small fee may apply)
  • Plant nursery with monthly sales of garden-cultivated plants
  • Botanical library and learning center
  • First aid station and security services
